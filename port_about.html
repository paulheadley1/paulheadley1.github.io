About Me

I’m Paul Headley — a retired service member with over 9 years of domestic/global service in high-stakes environments. Previously, I specialized in law enforcement operations, crisis response, and leadership development. My career has honed my skills in administration, training, resource-coordination, problem-solving, and team building.


As I pursue my B.A. in Information Technology & Informatics at Rutgers University, I’m focused on bridging my disciplined background with the ever-evolving world of technology. I’ve completed certifications in software development, with a strong foundation in core programming, OOP, web applications, and databases.


My mission is to leverage both worlds — military precision and tech innovation — to contribute to dynamic IT teams focused on growth, security, and smart systems. I take pride in collaboration, continuous learning, and achieving results that matter.
